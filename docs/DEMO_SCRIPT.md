# Five-Minute Winning Demo

## Scene 1 — The hidden champion
Open Shakti Precision Components. The firm has no bureau history, but GST and bank evidence reconcile, growth is positive and repayment capacity is healthy.

**Message:** no bureau history is missing evidence, not guilt.

## Scene 2 — Three scores, not one
Show Financial Health, Data Confidence and Resilience separately.

**Message:** a bank should know both the risk estimate and whether it has enough trustworthy evidence to rely on that estimate.

## Scene 3 — Evidence-linked explanation
Open the strongest positive driver and one negative concentration driver. Display the source and calculation.

**Message:** every conclusion is inspectable by a credit officer.

## Scene 4 — Stress-to-structure
Apply a 25% revenue decline and 45-day buyer delay. Show deterioration in DSCR and liquidity. Reduce the exposure or change the product until resilience returns.

**Message:** VYAPAR PULSE does not just reject the requested structure; it finds the safest viable structure.

## Scene 5 — Evidence gap abstention
Open NavPrerna Business Services. Data confidence is below threshold, so the engine refuses to recommend credit and requests specific missing evidence.

**Message:** responsible AI knows when not to decide.

## Scene 6 — Bankability conversion
Show the 30–90 day action plan: reconcile GST and bank flows, reduce buyer concentration, improve liquidity buffer and complete missing data.

**Closing line:** “VYAPAR PULSE converts fragmented data into explainable credit, safer structures and a measurable route from invisible to bankable.”
